Implement “Square-level” QuickBook handoff UX and enrich booking_intents.metadata on Book Now click.

GOAL
After lead capture, the widget shows:
- Bot: “Perfect — you’re all set. Tap Book Now to finish on our secure scheduling page.”
- Primary button: Book Now
- Secondary button: Talk to a Human (existing)
- Trust line: “You’ll finish on {ProviderName}. If you get interrupted, we’ll still have your request.”

On Book Now click, backend stores (merge into existing metadata):
- metadata.handoffClickedAt = new Date().toISOString()
- metadata.provider = intent.externalProviderName OR settings.externalBookingProviderName OR null
- metadata.handoffUrl = the url returned (external or demo)

NO SMS. NO new DB fields. NO new tables.
